Concentra Puzzle Solver

This app is based on the 1994 Concentra puzzle by the DaMert Company.

Try to solve it yourself by dragging the pieces into place or reveal the solutions panel for hints and more.

Note: A piece is correctly placed when its colors match the colors of the layers above and below it. Pieces in the same layer do not need to match.

They claim there is but a single solution...
